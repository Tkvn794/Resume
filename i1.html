Our Mission

At Save Burabay, we are committed to preserving the natural beauty and ecological health of the Burabay region. Our volunteer community brings together passionate individuals who share a vision of sustainable development and environmental stewardship.

What We Do

  • Environmental Initiatives: Organizing clean-ups of parks, lakes, and forests to reduce pollution and protect local biodiversity.
  • Community Outreach: Educating residents and visitors about the importance of conservation and responsible tourism.
  • Youth Engagement: Hosting workshops and mentorship programs to empower young people as future environmental leaders.

Join Our Efforts

Interested in making a difference? Whether you have a few hours or can commit long-term, your support helps our community thrive. Join us for upcoming events or contribute your skills in planning and outreach.
